These data show n=60 mice of both sexes. They are from 12 different litters. Their litter/dam id dam_id is given as well as two measures of their dams behavior - licking/grooming dam_lg and nursing dam_nurse. The weaning weight of each individual is given wean_wt as well as three measures of behavior of_inner, freezing & sociability, and two brain measures protein1, protein2.

The animals have two grouping variables also. An early treatment which they could either be in the control or stress group, and a post treatment, which they could either be in the control or treatment groups.
